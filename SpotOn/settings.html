Navigation Customisation
Custom color for NPB & NB
?
With this you can define any color, this will then be displayed on the Now Playing Bar, Nav bar, Now Playing View, Hotkeys, and Media Streamer

To try it out, you can use some simple colors like "pink" or, pick an Rgba/Hex/Hsl color!

Alternatively, you can use an Image OR GIF simply put the link in the box, you can also use a gradient!

Try this gradient~ linear-gradient(to bottom left, #FFB6C1, #FFD700, #87CEEB, #98FB98, #DDA0DD, #FFA500)

Once you have a color, press "Save Options" and reload Spotify!
